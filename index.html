About Me

My name is Ndou Mukhathutsheli Charles. I was born on December 25, 2003. I am a male South African, born in Mukula Village in Venda. I was raised by a single mother, along with my grandparents and my mother's siblings. My mother was unemployed for most of my childhood, even though she had qualifications. She only found employment when I started university. This situation inspired me to work hard and become successful, aiming to lift my family out of the effects of unemployment.

Growing up was not easy. Although we had our basic needs met, there was still much we lacked, and even obtaining basic necessities required a lot of hard work. My passion for computer sciences developed during my upbringing due to various influences and observing the world's situation. I attended primary school in my hometown, where I had to walk back and forth every day.
